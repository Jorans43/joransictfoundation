About Joran's ICT Foundation

Founded in 2023, Joran's ICT Foundation is a non-profit organization dedicated to empowering individuals and communities through information and communication technology.

We believe that access to technology and digital literacy are fundamental rights in today's world. Our mission is to bridge the digital divide by providing education, resources, and opportunities to those who need them most.

Since our inception, we've impacted over 5,000 lives through our various programs and initiatives.

Our Vision

Our Programs

We offer a variety of programs designed to meet the diverse needs of our community members at different stages of their digital journey.

Digital Literacy Program

Basic computer skills training for beginners, covering essential software, internet usage, and online safety.

  • 12-week course
  • Hands-on training
  • Certification upon completion
Learn More

Coding Bootcamp

Intensive training in web and mobile app development for aspiring tech professionals.

  • 6-month program
  • HTML, CSS, JavaScript
  • Job placement assistance
Learn More

Teacher Training

Equipping educators with digital tools and methodologies to enhance classroom learning.

  • 8-week workshop
  • EdTech integration
  • Ongoing support
